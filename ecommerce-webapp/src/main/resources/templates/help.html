Help

1. Contact customer service if you have any queries or you may drop us an email and we will respond within 3 working days. Thank you.



2. Please ensure that you are using a valid email and bank account number.



3. I would like to cancel my order. How can i do so?

 

Sorry no cancellation of order or refunds could be made once order is confirmed unless the product is damaged or order is lost.

4. Do you match prices if an item goes on sale after the purchase?

 

We do not change prices after an order is confirmed. Please stay tune to any sales.

5. Can i edit my email address?

 

Currently, we do not accept the change of email address. However, we are working to provide the edit of email address. We apologise for the inconvenience.
